Frequent questions

Here you'll be able find answers your questions

What is this Food Ordering System?

Our food ordering system is an online platform that allows customers to browse menus, place orders, and make payments conveniently from their homes or workplaces.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our menu, select your desired items, add them to the cart, and proceed to checkout to complete your order.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets, and cash on delivery.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it is prepared. Once the restaurant starts processing it, changes may not be possible.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you can track its status in real-time through our tracking feature.

What if I receive the wrong or missing items?

If you receive an incorrect or incomplete order, please contact our customer support immediately to resolve the issue.

Is there a delivery charge?

Delivery charges may apply based on your location and the restaurant's policy. Details will be provided during checkout.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or live chat available on our website.
